Our Mission

No human ever truly likes to be alone; it's in our nature to crave societal relationships and bonds. To be forcefully deprived of that is torture, especially when you're adolescent.

So why can't the same thing be said for fae? They talk like us, think like us, feel like us. That's what we're here for. Fae Thee Well is a podcast for the outcasts and misfits. The chipped and scrabbled. The wings and the dings. For people like you and me.

The monster in your closet wants to be heard, will you listen?
